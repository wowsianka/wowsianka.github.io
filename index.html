Project Deep Dive

Goldman Sachs

Deep Learning for CCR Exposure Prediction

+

An AI research initiative to accelerate CCR calculations by replacing slow Monte Carlo simulations with fast neural networks. Developing bidirectional LSTM networks to learn complex non-linear mappings between market risk factors and exposure metrics (EE, PFE).

  • Architecture: Bidirectional LSTM for sequential dependencies.
  • Inputs: Rates, vols, credit spreads, simulated MtM paths, and macro indicators.
  • Benefits: Orders of magnitude speedup, enabling real-time stress testing and what-if analysis.

Goldman Sachs

Stressed EAD Forecasting for Equities

+

Predictive model for daily stressed Exposure at Default under regulatory capital frameworks (Basel III / FRTB).

  • Approach: Linear regression on return-based features to handle non-stationarity.
  • Features: Market risk factor returns, weekly aggregates, and autoregressive lags.
  • Outcome: Supports daily risk reporting and proactive capital planning cycles.
